Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a Italian dish consisting of layers of flat pasta, alternating with fillings such as ragù (ground meat and tomato sauce), béchamel sauce, cheeses and spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground Meat
  • 1 medium Onion
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp fresh herbs
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp Spices and seasonings
  • 1/2 pound noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly.
  2. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  3. Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
  4. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  5. Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  6. Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.
