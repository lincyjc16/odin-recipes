French Toast

Description

French toast is a dish made from slices of bread dipped in a batter of beaten eggs and often milk or cream, then pan-fried until golden brown.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbsp of butter
  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp Vanilla

Steps

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  2. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  3. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  4. Serve hot.
